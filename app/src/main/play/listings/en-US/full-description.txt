When the Internet is unavailable, become a Relaynet courier and restore connectivity around you. Do it for profit, the greater good or both! This app is all you need.

Please note that at this point you won't be able to do much with this app until we've built and released the Relaynet app for end users.

To learn more about the job of a Relaynet courier and to subscribe to important updates, please visit https://relaynet.network/couriers
